🎵 YukkiMusic 🎶 ​

🔗 What is YukkiMusic? ​

YukkiMusic is a powerful and optimized version of the original
YukkiMusicBot, built for high-quality music streaming in Telegram voice chats using Python and Pyrogram.

🤝 Need Help? ​

We’ve got your back through multiple support channels:

  • 📝 Open a GitHub Issue
  • 💬 Contact Us on Telegram
  • 👥 Join the Support Group

❣️ Show Your Support ​
